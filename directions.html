Directions

From Atlanta:
Head east on I-20 E, toward Augusta
Take exit 138 for GA-77 toward Union Point/Sparta/GA-15/Siloam
Turn right at GA-77/GA-77 S, go 0.4 mi
Turn left at GA-15/GA-77/Greene County Memorial Hwy/Main St, toward Sparta
After about 7 miles, go through the town of White Plains. One mile south of White plains you will enter Hancock County. Three lane road at that point. After going back to two lanes, turn left on Edward Rd/Mt Stephens Church Rd. There is a sign for "Mt Stephens Church", when turning on Edward Rd
Go 0.7 mi, turn right. This is Gum Hill Rd.
After 0.2 mi, turn right at 1421 Gum Hill Rd.
