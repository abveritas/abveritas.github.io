Veritas Farm USA has ended, Anke Boersma returned to the Netherlands. It was located in White Plains, GA. The facility consisted of 60 acres, a covered arena (200 x 70) and a 220 x 100 outdoor arena. The following Services were offered:

  • Quality Dutch & Oldenburg Warmblood Horses
  • Dressage training & lessons
  • Stallion services
  • Handling for breed-shows
