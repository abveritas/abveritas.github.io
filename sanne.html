At Veritas Farm, there is always a nice selection of young Warmblood horses present. These horses are either imported as yearlings or two year olds, or bred at Veritas Farm. They are started under saddle in the spring in which they turn three. Please take a look at the current selection.

Sanne V
  • 1999, 17 hand, star rated KWPN mare, black
  • Pedigree: Sir Holtrup x Frühling xTriton
  • Succesfully competed FEI Dressage
Sanne V was the highest scoring mare in the USA at the 2004 NA/WPN keuring tour in free-jumping.
She also placed fifth in the USA at that tour in the DG Bar Cup (Dressage suitability class for young horses). In 2008 and 2010 she delivered a colt of Corcovado, while competing and winning up to FEI during the same period.
